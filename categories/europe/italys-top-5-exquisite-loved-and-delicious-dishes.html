Italy’s Top 5 Exquisite, Loved and Delicious Dishes

by Erickson Javier

Posted on March 27, 2018 at 10:03 PM

Everybody loves Italy as everybody loves Pizza, Spaghetti, Lasagna and everything pasta. As beautiful and elegant their country is, the food here is just simply marvelous. And mind you there is a LOT of this marvelous food. It’s just impossible to try everything that Italy has to offer, there is just an infinite number of Italian dishes that every foodie must absolutely try. But hey there is no need to for discouragement; we at Traveler’s Foodbox try our very best to give you our own top 5 list of Italian foods that we think everyone should at least try when they visit Italy.

1. Pizza

Well now there are two types of people in the world, first are the ones who like pizza and the other one is definitely lying. Well of course who doesn’t love pizza? There’s perhaps no better dish to represent the elegance and ingenuity of Italians as the humble pizza. Easy, cheap, and filling, pizza has long been a common snack or meal, and everybody just loves it. When in Italy it is very unwise to miss a slice of their majestic pizzas.

2. Lasagna

Pizzas are not the only the superstars in Italy. Lasagna also has the reputation of being very exquisite and pleasurable. Lasagna is a wide, flat pasta noodle, usually baked in layers in the oven. The balance between layers of cheese, noodles, and bolognese sauce is genius and just perfect. Italians make the most delicious and authentic tasting lasagna in the world and you could just never afford to miss it.

3. Risotto

Far off from the typical pasta cuisines from the list, we move to the one of the holy trinity of Italian starches which is rice. And no rice cuisine could be better than the Italian’s very own Risotto. This quintessential Italian dish is slow cooked short-grain Italian rice, the grains are perfect to absorb the flavour of the brilliantly seasoned broth and release starch to create the classic silky smooth texture. It is just creamy and luxurious to the taste. It’s like you win the lotto when you taste the Italian Risotto.

4. Carbonara

Another pasta dish that’s been around for a long time and acknowledged around the world for its elegance and taste is the Carbonara. And the credit all goes to the Italians for this dish is a must try. This cheesy pasta dish is simple yet difficult to learn and master. This exquisite dish is made with spaghetti, eggs, pecorino cheese, cured guanciale, and black pepper. Though there are many variations of the dish, only Italians make the best and authentic Carbonara in the world.

5. Gelato

To complete this list, we chose the widely loved dessert in Italy which is no other than the Gelato. No trip to Italy is complete without having a taste of gelato!. You might think “Oh this is just an Ice Cream, I could have just buy and eat it at home.” But unfortunately no, and sadly you are missing a lot. Ice cream and Gelato has the difference of day and night. By law, gelato has to have less butterfat than normal ice cream. The low-fat content means that gelato is served a bit warmer and tends to melt in your mouth faster; it also intensifies the flavor and gives it a smoother texture. Second, gelato has a much higher density. Normal ice cream has air and water added to increase volume and weight making it less flavorful. This is not practiced by Italians, leaving gelato super sweet and extra flavorful.
